The Top 100 Adjectives

Are there adjectives that appear more often in different tropes, and if so, what are they?

Examining the adjectives used in trope descriptions allows us to more easily understand the kinds of things being said about them. Here, we collect the top 100 most used adjectives. Among them we see examples of adjectives used to describe anything from physical appearance, such as ‘beautiful’ and ‘attractive’, to age, such as ‘young’ and ‘old’ and or personality traits such as ‘sympathetic’.

This visualization is best viewed on a larger screen. Check it out on a laptop or desktop.

 

Some adjectives tend to appear together commonly in different tropes. We wanted to see which adjectives were likely to appear together often. We connected adjectives that occur together in more than ten tropes using grey lines.
